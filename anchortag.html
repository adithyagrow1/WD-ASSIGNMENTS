Rolls-Royce Phantom is a luxury sedan car produced by Rolls-Royce Motor Cars, a subsidiary of BMW Group. It is considered one of the most expensive and luxurious vehicles in the world. The Phantom has been in production since 1926 and has undergone several generational changes over the years. The current version of the Phantom, the eighth generation, was unveiled in 2017 and features a modern, sleek design, as well as enhanced technology and performance.
